Ingredients

Syrup

  • 15.9 oz sugar
  • 12.7 oz water
  • 2.8 oz honey
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 orange peel
  • 1 stick cinnamon

Filling

  • 14 oz finely chopped walnuts
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves

Dough

  • 15.8 oz phyllo dough
  • 8.8 oz butter
  • 1.4 oz olive oil

Directions

  1. Add all syrup ingredients into a saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat and, when it begins to bubble, lower the heat and simmer for about 5 minutes. Remove from heat and cool to room temp.
  2. Mix all filling ingredients and set aside.
  3. Preheat oven to 320F. Place phyllo dough on cooking surface and cover to prevent it from drying out.
  4. Melt butter and mix with olive oil. Brush baking pan with a small amount of the mixture.
  5. Place 5 sheets of phyllo dough onto the pan and brush with mixture. Add about 1/5 of the filling on top of ther sheet, then add a few more phyllo dough sheets brushed with butter and olive oil on top. Continue until you are out of filling. Add remaining sheets on top and brush with remaining butter mixture.
  6. Divide into 3 strips, then cut into squares and half the squares diagonally (cutting a triangular shape).
  7. Bake for 1 hour and 30 minutes.
  8. Gently pour syrup on top of baklava after removing from the oven. Allow it to cool to room temp before adding to fridge.
  9. Serve with walnuts and honey. Enjoy!
