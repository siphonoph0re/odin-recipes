Dolmas (Stuffed Grape Leaves)

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 onions, minced
  • 1 1/2 cups uncooked white rice
  • 1 1/2 cups hot water
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons dried currants
  • 2 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon dried mint
  • 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
  • 1 tablespoon ground allspice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 8 oz jar grape leaves, drained and rinsed in warm water

Directions

  1. Heat oil in saucepan over medium heat. Add onions and saute about 5 minutes, or until tender. Stir in rice. Add enough hot water to cover. Put on lid, and simmer about 10 minutes, until rice is halfway cooked.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in tomato paste, currants, pine nuts, cinnamon, mint, dill, allspice, and cumin. Let cool.
  3. Place an inverted plate on the bottom of a large pot, this will protect the dolmas during steaming.
  4. Cut off grape leaf stems. Place about 1 teaspoon of cooled rice mixture into the center of a leaf. Folkd in the sides, then roll into cigar shape. Plate in pot. Repeat until you run out of mixture.
  5. Pour in just enough warm water to reach the bottom of the first layer of dolmas. Cover and simmer over low heat for 30 to 45 minutes, or until rice is totally cooked. Check the water level often and add more as necessary.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
