Ingredients

  • 2 lbs potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 4 zucchini, thinly sliced
  • 4 red onions, thinly sliced
  • 6 ripe tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Sea salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F.
  2. Spread potatoes, zucchini, and red onions in baking dish. Cover with pureed tomatoes, olive oil, and parsley. Add salt and pepper to your liking. Toss until evenly coated.
  3. Bake for 1 hour. Stir gently. Continue baking about 30 more minutes, until vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
